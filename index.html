Services

Hover over a service to learn more about what Deific Arts, LLC has to offer.

  • Design

    An important aspect of a good site is good design. Good design builds trust from your potential customers and keeps them engaged with your content. This increases the likelihood they’ll become your customers. Deific Arts, LLC promises to design a professional site that works well on all devices. You won’t be charged until you’re satisfied with the design.

  • Development

    Deific Arts, LLC builds sites primarily on WordPress which is a CMS (content management system). This allows you or your employees to make changes to the site as a user without needing technical coding knowledge. All of Deific Arts, LLC sites are custom builds using modern tech such as HTML5, CSS3, and ES6 and beyond.

  • eCommerce

    Small businesses often could benefit from having an online store but skip out on it due to the price. If you’re looking to sell goods online but don’t have the budget of over $25,000 that you may be charged by an agency then contact Deific Arts, LLC. As a small business, servicing other small businesses with simple store requirements is what Deific Arts, LLC focuses on.

  • SEO

    Search Engine Optimization is key to driving business to your site. A lot of developers build sites without measuring specifics when it comes to performance and SEO. Deific Arts, LLC uses the industry standard Lighthouse tool to ensure that your site is compliant with SEO best practices and performs well with fast load times.

  • Hosting

    Once your site is designed and developed it will need to live on the web. This is called hosting. Deific Arts, LLC handles hosting for you so there’s no need to worry about signing up for a hosting provider with complicated and confusing terminology. Unless of course you want to self host!

  • Training

    Once your site is live, Deific Arts, LLC provides training and support on how to use your product. The use of a CMS will allow you to do many updates on your own but you will always have support with Deific Arts, LLC at your side.
